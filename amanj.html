Welcome All
I am a research scholar, associated with Malgudi Labs (Dr SRS Iyengar) in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Ropar

Prior to joining IIT Ropar, I did my Masters from IIT Jodhpur and had a wonderful time there. This institute gave me lots of friends, concepts and of course so many respected faculties.

Before moving to Jodhpur, I spent about three years in ‘Silicon Valley of India’, Bengaluru as a software engineer in TATA Consultancy Services Ltd. I had a life changing experience in this Hi-Tech city. It contributed to all walks of my personal and professional life. I did my bachelors from Guru Nanak Dev University Amritsar. This was the golden period of my life. Amritsar is known as heritage city of Punjab .It is 28 Kms away from Lahore Pakistan .It holds special place in the history of Punjab.

I was born in bathinda district of Punjab. For better education I moved to city and started living with my maternal grandparents at a very young age. I finished my initial studies from Sri Guru Harkrishan Public School Bathinda.
