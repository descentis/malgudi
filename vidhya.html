About Me
I am a research scholar @ Malgudi Labs under Dr. Sudarshan Iyengar, department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Ropar. I finished my BTech. (2013-2017 batch) in Computer Science and Engineering from SRM University, Chennai. After my BTech., I joined PhD in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Ropar, in August 2017.

I hail from a place called Chidambaram in Cuddalore District, TamilNadu, India. Being the only daughter, I always got great love and support from my family. My lovely family comprises of my father who is an Associate Professor in Chemical Engineering and my mother who is currently a home-maker. Before marriage she worked as a teacher. Coming from such a family of academicians motivated me to pursue my career towards research and academia. The thing that I like the most about this profession is that it helps you stay connected to your concepts as you are continuosly brushing them up. Also I find that academicians feel young as they constantly move with youngsters. In addition to staying connected with scratch concepts it is also essential to keep oneself updated. This is possible through research. Hence I opted Ph.D which according to me is a journey of making oneself ready into the enthralling world of teaching and research.

Hobbies:
My hobbies include listening to music, reading books, watching movies, etc.To know more about me just be with me for sometime. You would find a simple, fun loving, positive person around you.
